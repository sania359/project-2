About Our Planet

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet that supports life. About 71% of Earth's surface is covered with water, while the remaining 29% consists of land. It has a protective atmosphere rich in oxygen, making life possible. Earth rotates once every 24 hours, creating day and night, and revolves around the Sun in about 365 days. It is home to millions of living species and diverse ecosystems including forests, deserts, mountains, rivers, and oceans. Conserving nature, reducing pollution, and protecting natural resources are essential to keeping our planet healthy for future generations.
