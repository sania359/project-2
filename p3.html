Lal Qila (Red Fort) is a historic 17th-century Mughal fortress in Old Delhi, India. Commissioned by Emperor Shah Jahan in 1639 when moving his capital from Agra to Delhi, the striking red sandstone complex served as the main royal residence and continues to host the Prime Minister's annual Independence Day address.
